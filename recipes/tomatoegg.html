Tomato and egg recipe

Description

Tomato and egg is made of Tomato and egg. Crazy right. It's bonkers.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb tomato
  • 4 egg
  • 5 slices ginger
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch (for slurry)
  • 4 tbsp water (for slurry)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 3 tbsp water
  • 0.50 tsp salt (for eggs)
  • 0.50 tsp salt (for stir-fry)
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar (for stir-fry)
  • 2 tbsp ketchup (for stir-fry)

Steps

  1. Cut tomatoes into wedges and remove stem
  2. Cut ginger
  3. Prepare eggs and season with salt
  4. Prepare the cornstarch slurry
  5. cook the eggs and stir
  6. cook the tomatoes and season with salt and brown sugar
  7. Add the eggs, ketchup and stir
  8. Taste and serve, enjoy!
