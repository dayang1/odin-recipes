Congee Recipe

Description

Congee or conjee is a type of rice porridge or gruel eaten in Asian countries. When eaten as plain rice congee, it is most often served with side dishes.

Ingredients

  • 6 oz rice
  • 8 cups water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp oil
  • 3 century egg
  • 0.50 lb pork butt
  • 1 oz dried scallop
  • 0.25 oz ginger

Steps

  1. Prepare rice and dried scallops
  2. Heat water and start to cook rice and scallops together
  3. Prepare pork
  4. Marinate pork and chop garnishes
  5. Stir the congee occasionally
  6. Add the pork and century egg
  7. Dish is ready to serve, enjoy!
